Welcome to my Homepage !

I am a Radio astronomer (French national) working at
University of Lyon and
Chair of IAU Women in Astronomy Working Group International Astronomical Union



Research Interest:
Cluster of galaxies, Bright cluster galaxy, Distant Universe, Lensing, SZ effect, Dark Matter, distant radio emitting galaxies, Star Forming galaxies, AGNs, variability in radio jets, observational astronomy, Instrumentation


Science Projects (ongoing): using data from GMRT, NRT, NenuFAR, LOFAR, VLA, WSRT, EVN, IRAM, SKA, HST, MUSE

-Science and Commissioning with NenuFAR- French SKA Pathfinder (For recent results- refer publication list)
-Non thermal emission in large scale structures (Galaxy clusters and Giant radio galaxies) of the Universe and their multi wavelength (X-ray, Optical, IR) properties
-HI & CO observations of galaxies
-Low frequency emission in AGNs and their optical hosts, classification, SFR
-Variability in radio jets
-Evolutionary Map of the Universe (EMU) - Australian SKA Pathfinder (ASKAP)

PI and Consortium Membership:
LOFAR Continuum survey on Cluster of galaxies and AGNs
LOFAR MSSS survey on Cluster of galaxies and AGNs
LOFAR-WEAVE survey
ASKAP Continuum and WALLABY-HI survey
SKA Continuum survey on Cluster of galaxies and AGNs and SKA VLBI
IRAM suvery on Cluster of galaxies
NRT HI survey on Clusters of galaxies
GMRT HI and Continuum survey on Clusters of galaxies and AGNs

Teaching and student projects:
I give Bachelor's level courses (in French and English!) on Astronomy and Astrophysics, Scientific English, Optics, Classical Mechanics, Thermodynamics at University Claude Bernard Lyon 1
and regularly offer short research projects to graduate students.
